Memory Span Test

Forward Digit Recall

🧠 Ready to challenge your memory?

Let's test your working memory! This test measures how many numbers you can remember and recall in the correct order. It's a fun way to assess your short-term memory capacity! 💭

📋 How It Works:

  • A sequence of random digits will appear on screen, one at a time 🎲
  • After the sequence, type the numbers in the exact same order you saw them ⌨️
  • Press the # key to submit your answer ✅
  • Sequences start short and get progressively longer 📈
  • You get 2 attempts at each length – fail both and the test ends 🎯
